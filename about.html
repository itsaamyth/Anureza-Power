Who We Are ?

Anureza Power is a fully E.P.C. Company serving the utility scale solar photo voltaic market along with 33/11 kV-HT/LT substations and transmission. Our corporate office is located in Jasola, New-Delhi (INDIA) with operations team spread out across India. We leverage our experience and expertise to provide high quality services to our clients. We have a self-performance appraisal mechanism which allows us to outperform ourselves and competitors in terms of quality, delivery and value. We as a company strongly believe maximizing customer value is paramount and the lasting relationships that we build with our clients allow us to mold our services around the needs of our customers.
